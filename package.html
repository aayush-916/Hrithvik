Your Gateway to Adventure in the Majestic Himalayas. Explore breathtaking landscapes, uncover ancient treasures, and embrace warm hospitality.


Kathmandu

Kathmandu, Nepal's capital, is a vibrant city with ancient temples, palaces, and stupas. Its bustling streets, traditional architecture, and lively markets blend rich history with modern life.

Pokhara

Pokhara, Nepal, is scenic city known for its natural beauty. Near the Annapurna range, boasts Phewa Lake, Devi's Fall, and the World Peace Pagoda, attract adventurers and nature lovers.

Inquiry

Chitwan

Chitwan, in southern Nepal, is famous for Chitwan National Park, a UNESCO site with diverse wildlife. It offers jungle safaris, bird watching, and Tharu cultur experiences, attract eco-tourism.

BIHAR

Your Gateway to Adventure in the Heart of Bihar. Explore historic landscapes, uncover ancient treasures, and embrace warm hospitality.


Rajgir

Rajgir, in Bihar, India, is a historic city surrounded by lush green hills, known for its ancient ruins, hot springs, and Gridhakuta hill. It holds spiritual significance for Buddhists and Jains, offering a serene environment with rich history and numerous cultural landmarks.

Inquiry

Bodhgaya

Bodhgaya, Bihar, India, is a key Buddhist pilgrimage site where Siddhartha Gautama attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree. The Mahabodhi Temple complex, a UNESCO World Heritage site, attracts devotees and visitors with its profound spiritual and historical import.

Inquiry

Patna

Patna, the capital of Bihar, India, is a historic city with a vibrant blend of old and new. Its bustling streets, historical monuments like Golghar, and lively markets reflect a rich cultural heritage and dynamic urban life, showcasing diverse traditions and contemporary growth.

Inquiry

Pawapuri

Pawapuri, in Bihar, India, is a sacred Jain pilgrimage site renowned for the Jal Mandir, its beautiful marble temple, tranquil water surroundings, and its profound historical significance.

Inquiry

Nalanda

Nalanda is a historical site in Bihar, India, renowned for its ancient university ruins, rich cultural heritage, and serene landscapes. Explore centuries-old architecture and learn about its scholarly past.

Inquiry

Vaishali

Vaishali, in Bihar, India, is an ancient city with rich historical and cultural heritage, famous for its archaeological ruins, Ashokan Pillar, and significance in Buddhist and Jain traditions.
